## PITCH DECK TEMPLATE (10 Slides)

Based on Jason Della Rocca's GDC Pitch framework:

1. **Title Slide** - Game name, studio, tagline
2. **The Hook** - What makes it unique (Hangman + Battleship, asymmetric difficulty)
3. **Gameplay Overview** - Core mechanics explained
4. **Target Audience** - Word game fans, families, casual strategy
5. **Comparables** - Wordle + Battleship + Hangman
6. **Current Status** - Playable demo, features complete
7. **Team** - Your background, TecVooDoo LLC
8. **Business Plan** - Platforms, timeline, ask
9. **Traction** - Demo plays, feedback, press (if any)
10. **Contact** - How to reach you

## WHAT TO SAY IN YOUR PITCH

**Subject Line Example:**
"Don't Lose Your Head - Hangman meets Battleship word puzzle (playable demo)"

**Opening Hook:**
"Don't Lose Your Head is a competitive word puzzle game combining Hangman's letter-guessing with Battleship's hidden grid strategy. Players hide words on grids and race to find their opponent's words before the guillotine blade falls."

**Key Differentiator:**
"Our asymmetric difficulty system lets players of different skill levels compete fairly - a parent and child can play together with the experienced player handicapping themselves."

**Call to Action:**
"Play the demo at dlyh.pages.dev. I'd love to discuss how [Publisher Name] might be a good fit for Don't Lose Your Head."
